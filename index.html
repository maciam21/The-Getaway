A MINIMALIST ARTICLE

“That’s been one of my mantras—focus and simplicity. Simple can be harder than complex: You have to work hard to get your thinking clean to make it simple. But it’s worth it in the end because once you get there, you can move mountains.” —Steve Jobs

By: Miguel Angel Macias Flores

Why is this tittled the "The Getaway"? Good question! Besides the fact that it shares the same title to one of my favorite Red Hot Chilli Peppers song and album, it has always been my sacred phrase to tittle places that make me feel safe, relaxed and at peace. With that in mind, I recently came upon the term "minimalism" and everything it represents. I have to thank my girlfriend for pointing out the biggest elephant in the room; I realized it when she explained that I was a minimalist. Not only was I intrigued.. I was fascinated!

When you live a minimalist lifestyle, you try to use only things that have a function. It's all about living simply and having only what you need to get by each day. For some people, this may embark them on a "no-spend" challenge or limit their purchases to only what they absolutely require.

The idea for this article came from a recent intereaction that I had with a close friend of mine. I really wanted to show my progress

To put my thoughts together on paper...

The idea behind this article is to put all the minimalistic practices I came across and put it into play to your everyday lifestyle; in hopes to provide a postive recommendation in a fancy To- Do-List. Of course with really cool illustrations! Best preactices on what worked best and what to avoid..or not.
